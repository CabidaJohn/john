Concert Safety Tips

  • Stay Hydrated

    -Always carry water with you and take breaks to rehydrate.

  • Be aware of your surroundings

    -Familiarize yourself with the venue layout. Know where emergency exits, medical stations, and restrooms are located.

  • Buddy system

    - Stick with your friends throughout the event. Keep an eye on each other and agree on a meeting point in case you get separated.

  • Moderate Noise Exposure

    -Consider wearing earplugs to protect your hearing. The loud music at EDM concerts can pose risks to your ears over time.
