FAQs

Q: Where can i go to refill my hydro pack?

A: Look near the edges of the venue towards the vendors and food areas with the huge containers to refill your hydropack!

Q: I lost my light up bucket hat! Is there some way i can find it?

A: Yes! you can find a lost and found tent near the security gate to find or submit a claim for missing belongings!

Q: Are Earplugs necessary for the concert?

A: Yes, Earplugs provide protection to prevent hearing loss. A long period of time without them could damage your ears

Q: What is the EDM community like?

A: Most of the community follows an acronym called P.L.U.R which stands for Peace, Love, Unity, and Respect. All you need to feel welcome is to bring positive vibes and be respectful towards others.
